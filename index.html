Flexibility playbook

Search month, not day. Start with "whole month" view on Google Flights / Skyscanner.
Multi-city often beats 2× one-way. Open-jaw (e.g. HEL→BKK, DAC→HEL) routes through one hub can be 20–30% cheaper than two return trips.
Incognito mode. Repeated searches on the same route can drift prices up.
Tue/Wed beats Fri/Sun. 12–22% lower on Schengen, up to 30% on long-haul.
Alternate airports. RIX, TLL, ARN — a €30 ferry can shave €200 off long-haul.
